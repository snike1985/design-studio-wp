VV Supremo

ux ui visual

Founded in 1964, VV Supremo is one of the most prolific US producers of cheeses and other dairy products. This company of over 240 employees has been a market leader for more than 50 years.

The company’s old website didn’t match the strong VV Supremo brand. It also had multiple UX aspects that needed to be improved.

GOALS

A few things we want users to do on the website:

  • browse perfect fresh cheeses and other groceries;
  • get access to the full catalog as well as nutrition information,
  • quickly find directions to the nearest brick and mortar and
  • view, utilize and share recipes.

VV Supremo’s wildly popular Youtube channel boasts dozens of thousands of views.

PROTOTYPING

Although original prototypes developed by the client were tweaked and updated many times, those prototypes were extremely helpful in terms of understanding the client’s vision and expectations for the layout and general look and feel of the web project.

If you are planning to launch your own web project, I highly recommend starting with wireframes, whether if they are created in a specialized app or just by hand. This seemingly simple process will have a huge impact on your understanding of how the project will function. It will also save you a lot of time and money on both design and development.

VISUALS

VV Supremo recipes are the most sharable content on the website. To draw more attention to the recipes we have designed various themes for different categories.

RESULT

In collaboration with the great Got.It team and the client’s marketing department, we were able to design a fresh and vibrant website built on modern coding technologies. The site is intended to emphasize the company’s progressive insights while honoring its traditions.
